India



Donate Now

A study shows that 4 percent of the Indian population, or 20 million children, are orphans. The high proportion of abandoned children among orphans highlights the fact that poverty is a major reason behind the situation.

According to a Supreme Court PIL, over 11 million children were abandoned in India in 2013, most of whom were girls. Many orphans have simply have been put into foster care by families who find it hard to take care of them.

We need your support in order to help these children. Every small contribution that you make will have a direct benefit on the lives of the orphans.
